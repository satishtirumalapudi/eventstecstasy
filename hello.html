Webkooks

So, what are you waiting for! Come, participate and give us the best you can. See you all there!

Code-a-thon
CODE-A-THON is an event of TECSTASY 2k18 organized as a part of SUDHEE by IT department of CBIT. Each Team will have to choose a problem statement to which they will be working on a solution. This involves 2 days(approx 30hrs) of hard coding . The solution can be in the form of a Web/Progressive Web/Android App. Later, each team should give a presentation on their work.

Bug Hunter
Have the capability to detect bugs in the code, starting from a semicolon!? Well, TECSTASY is a perfect arena to showcase your skills. BUG HUNTER is an event of TECSTASY 2k18 organized as a part of SUDHEE by IT department of CBIT. Bug Hunter is a debugging event in which you can discover the debugger in you. Don't wait until you have a bug to step through your code. Hunt the bugs in the given time and become the ultimate King or Queen of bug hunters. You can choose your interested language of programming to debug. Solve the set of questions given to you in a time of 20 min If writing code is an art, debugging is black magic.

Smart coding
Are you brave enough to enter the room?. SMART CODING is an event of TECSTASY 2k18 organized as a part of SUDHEE by IT department of CBIT. A coding competition where contestants are trapped in a room for a certain amount of time in which they will be given a mix of logical reasoning and programming based questions.The trick here is to find the answer to get the next clue.Each step will get you and your team closer to the key.Find the key by answering all the questions before time runs out!. All participants will be rewarded and the top scorers will be given merit certificates apart from cash prizes. So are you up for the challenge ?
